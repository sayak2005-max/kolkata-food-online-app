About Taste of Kolkata Food

Welcome to Taste of Kolkata, where the vibrant flavors of Kolkata Food come alive! Our restaurant is a celebration of the city's rich culinary heritage, bringing you authentic Bengali dishes. Established in 2025, we aim to create an unforgettable dining experience that blends the warmth of Bengali hospitality with modern comfort. Our ingredients are sourced fresh from local markets, ensuring every bite captures the essence of Kolkata’s bustling food culture.

Join us to savor the soul of Bengal, right here in the heart of the city!
